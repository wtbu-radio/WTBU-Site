Zach Flint, formerly a DJ for Very Shallow Listening on WTBU, compiled a list of his top albums and songs of 2017.

 

Top Albums of 2017

1. LCD Soundsystem - American Dream

2. Ariel Pink - Dedicated to Bobby Jameson

3. Rina Sawayama - Rina

4. Tyler, The Creator - Flower Boy

5. The Caretaker - Everywhere at the End of Time

6. Converge - The Dusk in Us

7. Circuit des Yeux - Reaching for Indigo

8. Broken Social Scene - Hug of Thunder

9. Love Theme - Love Theme

10. Laurel Halo - Dust
